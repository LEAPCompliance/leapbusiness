Description of Services

LEAP Business Solutions provides labour law compliance consultancy services to businesses across India, including but not limited to:

  • PF / EPF Compliance: ECR filing, challan generation, UAN management, and annual returns under the Employees' Provident Fund & Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952
  • ESIC Compliance: Monthly contribution filing, IP registration, claim assistance, and returns under the ESIC Act, 1948
  • Professional Tax (PT): State-wise deduction, payment, and return filing
  • Labour Welfare Fund (LWF): Contribution management and filing for applicable states
  • Payroll Management: End-to-end salary processing, TDS, and statutory deductions
  • Labour Law Audit: Compliance gap assessment and statutory audit services
  • Free Compliance Audit: A complimentary initial audit offered to prospective clients
